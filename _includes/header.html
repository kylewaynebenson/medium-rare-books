About the Intercom Brand Studio Library

Our library began in April 2018 with the aquisition of a chunk of rare books from Dave Cuzner.

Why do we have our own library?

We’re constantly bombarded on a daily basis by visual “inspiration”. New websites, rebrands etc. It can be overwhelming and tough to critically assess what parts of that can influence our work. That inspired me to think of ways that we can surface inspiration material that just doesn’t exist online. And though it is from years gone by, still influences design today. We should have access to that material.

The act of picking a book, sitting with it, enjoying it and absorbing helps us slow down and form a more thoughtful perspective on where design has been, and how it can shape our current and future work. It also acts as a counter balance to so much of the trend driven work that surrounds us.
